About

Anthony
Leuterio

His natural charismatic personality and wit to maximize different effective channels in marketing and selling brought this man-of-the-hour in real estate sector in the Philippines to the top.

Anthony Gerard Orais Leuterio, the president and founder of several companies under the Leuterio Realty & Brokerage Group has made real estate in the Philippines more prominent and much attractive to buyers. His out-of-the-box innovations in property selling reach to broader spectrum of real estate buyers around the world utilizing the social media and other online channels.

Doing only what he loves to do, Mr. Leuterio took the world of real estate industry by surprise, as award giving bodies accorded several awards to his exemplary initiatives since he decided to enter into real estate business.

He has been a recipient of over 1,000 awards and accolades. He is the first E-pro Certified Broker in the Philippines certified by National Association of Realtors.

One of his brainchild innovations is the establishment of the biggest rental website called Rent.ph, which is now helping millions of customers needing for rent properties, as well as property owners offering rental services.

Leuterio has inspired many individuals and has helped change the lives of many real estate brokers and agents. To date, his little brokerage empire is giving livelihood to nearly 15,000 people all over the country.

From a small office in Cebu City, Mr. Leuterio's inspirational social media prowess and charismatic leadership have been instrumental in opening offices in Mandaue City, Talisay City, Lapulapu City and even in Manila, Camsur, Legaspi, Cavite, Laguna, Bohol, Iligan, Cagayan de Oro, Davao, General Santos City, Butuan, Bacolod, Iloilo, Roxas, Dumaguete, Catbalogan, Calbayog and Ormoc.
